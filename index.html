Prosthetic Hand Project

Welcome to our innovative prosthetic hand project, a cutting-edge solution designed to improve the lives of individuals with upper limb differences. Our project combines advanced EMG technology with practical functionality to create an accessible and affordable prosthetic solution.

Project Features & Goals

Advanced Technology

Utilizing EMG (Electromyography) sensors to detect muscle signals and convert them into precise finger movements. This technology allows for intuitive control and natural hand movements.

Affordability & Accessibility

Designed with cost-effectiveness in mind, making advanced prosthetic technology accessible to a wider range of users. We focus on creating solutions that are both high-quality and affordable.

Customization & Comfort

Each prosthetic hand can be customized to fit the user's specific needs and measurements. We prioritize comfort and functionality in our design process.

Educational Value

Serves as an educational tool for students and researchers in biomedical engineering, robotics, and related fields. The project provides hands-on experience with cutting-edge technology.

Technical Specifications

Our prosthetic hand features advanced EMG sensors that detect muscle signals with high precision. The design includes: • 5 individually controllable fingers • Responsive EMG signal processing • Durable and lightweight materials • Rechargeable battery system • User-friendly interface • Customizable grip patterns

The system utilizes advanced algorithms to process EMG signals and translate them into precise finger movements, enabling natural and intuitive control for users.
